# Ali - Mental Health Companion App

## Product Vision
AI-powered mental health platform combining Duolingo engagement, Apple design, and therapy warmth.

## Target Users
- Young adults 18-35 experiencing stress, anxiety, emotional confusion
- Users wanting emotional support without immediate therapy
- People in therapy wanting daily tracking tools

## Main Features Implemented

### A. Onboarding Flow
- User name, age range
- Emotional goals selection
- Communication style preference
- Personalized welcome from Ali

### B. AI Emotional Assistant
- Warm, empathetic chat
- Reflective questions
- Crisis detection and support flow
- Adaptive tone based on user profile

### C. Daily Emotional Check-in
- Mood (5 emoji options)
- Energy, stress, anxiety sliders
- Sleep quality
- Notes
- Post check-in reflection from AI

### D. Progress Tracking
- Mood evolution charts
- Streaks
- Weekly summaries
- AI-generated insights

### E. Guided Exercises
- Breathing exercises
- Grounding techniques
- Journaling prompts
- Anxiety reduction
- Sleep routines

### F. Emotional Education
- Learning paths (anxiety, self-esteem, relationships, etc.)
- Progress tracking
- Milestones and rewards

### G. Professional Booking
- Therapist profiles
- Specialty filters
- Booking calendar
- Appointment history

### H. Journal
- Free writing
- Guided prompts
- Emotion tags
- Search entries

## Safety Features

- Emergency resources display
- Clear AI boundaries (not a therapist)
- Privacy-first approach
